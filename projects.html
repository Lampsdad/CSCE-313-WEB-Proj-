PiHole

The PiHole is a DNS-level Adblocker designed to be run on a Raspberry Pi. Blocking Ads and trackers before they ever make it to your device. I spent the time to set up a PiHole instance on AWS for my friends and me to use. Since the software operates at the DNS level, I had to allow only connections from a private VPN I control to prevent the cloud instance from being used for reflected DDOS attacks.

RSS Aggregator

I developed a script that scans preset websites for updates made to their RSS feeds. This script then interfaces with a NodeJS webserver, which will automatically push an update to a client's web client. Allowing for real-time news updates that can be further defined into different subcategories.

SAE Aero

I was a member of the SAE Aero design team, managing their website and helping to secure additional funding for the club. Being on the management side of two design teams I sometimes had to step in to help with the coding process for simulations, managing our connection with the HPRC office on campus and helping with the controllers we were using for competition.
