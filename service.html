Community Service

Tamu Gardens: My favorite place to give back to my community at college has been volunteering in the TAMU Gardens, It is a place where I have volunteered on and off working on projects to maintain the different sections over the years. They have volunteer programs in maintenance, planting, event outreach, etc.. and the beauty of the gardens makes every experience enjoyable. Big Event: Something that I plan on joining this year is the Texas A&M Big Event Program. A huge one-day “thank you” to the local College Station Community organized entirely by students. In the past, this event has always coincided poorly with my exam schedules, but knowing people on the executive board, I have made the commitment to join this year.
